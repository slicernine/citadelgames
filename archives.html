Year 330. The children of the galaxy shapers have lived and grown in a world of unity, peace, and progress.

In year 309, an Empire and Republic were once again established in the Skyriver Galaxy. After a grueling war, a controversial tactic of a Jedi General named Zema Sebatyne landed the Republic with several key core planets occupied of the Empire's; in quick retaliation, Darth Marr attacked the Republic Core, occupying key systems akin to those taken on their side. In a state of confusion and panic, the two sides drafted a quick peace to spare the lives of their homeworld citizens, but neither side were willing to give up what they had taken. Stuck in foreign lands, three sith stalks the halls of the Jedi Temple on Coruscant, looking to make their mark.
